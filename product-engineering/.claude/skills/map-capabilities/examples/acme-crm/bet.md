## Option chosen

A purpose-built CRM for mid-market B2B teams that models company relationship graphs
(not just flat contacts) and surfaces pipeline intelligence from activity patterns.
Displaces generic CRM by making company-level context a first-class construct.

## Rationale

Generic CRMs treat contacts as the core entity; mid-market B2B teams sell to
buying committees across organisations. The relationship graph capability is the
differentiator that unlocks cross-contact pipeline reasoning. Building it enables
a product moat that buy-and-customise paths cannot match within the appetite.

## Risks accepted

- Relationship graph requires novel data modelling; team has no prior art here.
- Email delivery and contact storage are bought/adopted to contain scope.

## Assumptions

- Mid-market B2B buyers prioritise pipeline visibility over feature breadth.
- Sufficient public company data APIs exist to seed graph enrichment.

## Kill condition

If graph enrichment accuracy falls below 70% after two quarters, pivot to a
simpler flat-contact model (reducing build scope by ~40%).
