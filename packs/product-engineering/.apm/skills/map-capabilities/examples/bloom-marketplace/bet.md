## Option chosen

A B2C marketplace for independent makers and small brands. Differentiates on two
axes: (1) a high-quality seller onboarding and compliance process that keeps catalogue
quality high, and (2) search and discovery that surfaces niche products general
marketplaces suppress.

## Rationale

Commodity payments and catalogue storage are widely available and not worth building;
the investment goes into onboarding and search — the two levers buyers cite for why
they prefer curated marketplaces over general ones.

## Risks accepted

- Search relevance is a long-feedback-loop problem; meaningful quality signal takes
  months of transaction data.
- Seller onboarding quality gates may slow early supply growth.

## Assumptions

- Payment gateway can be adopted (Stripe or equivalent) without meaningful
  customisation within the appetite window.
- Sellers will tolerate a 3–5 day onboarding review in exchange for higher buyer traffic.
